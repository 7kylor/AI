Applications and Innovations of

Artificial Intelligence



Artificial intelligence (AI) has a broad variety of applications, ranging from aerospace technology to banking and finance, biology, emergency services, music, the military, retail packaging, and industrial assembly and design. AI's specialised topics include an expert system for diagnosing manufacturing equipment, an inventory management tool, a designer's helper for cooling systems, and a knowledge-based integrated circuit critic. A connector assembly specification expert system was used to gather precise information about the assembly process, as well as a mixed initiative approach to airlift planning and decision aids for naval combat management.
